A bit about me

I'm a fullstack builder who ships — software systems, ML products, and product engineering from idea to production. At Uber I build the platforms and models behind competitive market intelligence. Outside of building, I'm passionate about giving back — whether it's mentoring early-career engineers, judging hackathons, or just having honest conversations about navigating tech careers. If you're figuring things out, I'm always happy to chat.
